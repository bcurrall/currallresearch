Welcome to the Currall Research Website

Welcome to my website. Within you will find a brief overview of my research, highlighting many of my research interests.

Convergent Genomics

At the heart of my research is the pursuit to understand the genetic basis of disease. By examining the the human genome, we will gain insight into this blue print of life, hopefully leading to dianosis and treat diseases like hearing loss and prostate cancer.

Hearing Loss

Hearing loss is one of the most prevelant diseases in the developed world. Since the comencement of my research career, I have investigated the fundamental physiology and the underlying genetic etiology of this devastating disease.

Prostate Cancer

Prostate cancer is the most prevelant cancer in men. After personal experience and the our discovery of a syndromic metabolic disease which causes both hearing loss and prostate cancer, my research nas progressed to the pursuit of metabolic pathology.
